For this father-son trip, prioritize either River North/Loop convenience or West Loop food access. I would avoid compact one-bed hotels unless you are fully comfortable sharing one bed.

Best balanced: Hyatt Place Chicago/River North Good first quote to check: central, practical, rooms with sofa-bed options, easy for steakhouse and river cruise days.

Draft Day Plan

This is intentionally light until hotels are known. It keeps Tuesday away from the Art Institute because the museum currently lists Tuesday as closed.

Sun 4 OctArrival
TRD-CPH-ORD, then easy evening

After Terminal 5 immigration and baggage, choose hotel transfer and a low-risk dinner. If staying downtown, Prime & Provisions or Green Street Smoked Meats are good arrival-night targets.

Mon 5 OctArchitecture
Chicago River, Loop, Millennium Park

Do the architecture river cruise while it is still in the late-season window, then walk the Riverwalk, Chicago Cultural Center, Millennium Park, and the lakefront.

Tue 6 OctScience
Griffin Museum of Science and Industry

Best teen-friendly museum day: U-505 Submarine, science exhibits, giant dome theater, and Hyde Park or Chinatown food afterward.

Wed 7 OctArt + city
Art Institute, Route 66 start, West Loop dinner

Use Wednesday for the Art Institute and downtown icons. Evening choice: Au Cheval burger, Bavette's steak, or a show if schedules line up.

Thu 8 OctMuseum campus
Field Museum and lakefront

Field Museum, Shedd/Alder Planetarium viewpoints, then a relaxed steakhouse night. Thursday also works as an Art Institute backup because of later hours.

Fri 9 OctNorth Side
Wrigleyville, Lincoln Park, comedy/theatre check

Use the North Side for Wrigley Field, Lincoln Park, Old Town, or Second City/Chicago Theatre only if the specific show feels suitable for a 17-year-old.

Sat 10 OctCar day
Best car-rental day

Pick one driving routine: Oak Park + Morton Arboretum for architecture/fall colors, or Indiana Dunes for lake scenery and a real road-trip feel.

Sun 11 OctDepart
Last morning, then ORD

Keep the day close to the hotel. Aim for ORD around 16:00 for the 19:15 long-haul departure, earlier if returning a rental car.

Driving Routines

For this trip, the strongest recommendation is a one-day rental, not a week-long rental. CTA and walking are better inside downtown Chicago.

Routine A: Oak Park + Morton Arboretum Architecture, Frank Lloyd Wright streets, fall color, manageable mileage. Best all-round car day for 10 Oct.
Open Route
Routine B: Indiana Dunes Lake Michigan beaches, dunes, industrial-skyline contrast, and a stronger "road trip" feel. Weather-dependent.
Open Route
Routine C: North Shore short loop Bahai Temple, Evanston lakefront, Northwestern campus, and easy return. Good if you want a shorter driving day.
Open Route
Car rental timing If returning the car on 11 Oct, leave a generous buffer. Rental return plus international check-in can turn a relaxed day into airport arithmetic.
